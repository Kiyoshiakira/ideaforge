Resources for Artists

Discover powerful free and open-source software for digital artists, designers, writers, animators, musicians, and more. Click a tool’s name to visit its official site and download.

Digital Painting & Illustration

  • Krita (Win/Mac/Linux) – Professional open-source painting & illustration.
  • MediBang Paint (Win/Mac/iOS/Android) – Lightweight, comic-focused painting.
  • FireAlpaca (Win/Mac) – Simple, beginner-friendly digital painting.

Photo Editing & Graphic Design

  • GIMP (Win/Mac/Linux) – Powerful image manipulation & photo editor.
  • Photopea (Web) – Online editor (works with PSD/XCF files).
  • Inkscape (Win/Mac/Linux) – Vector graphics (like Illustrator).
  • Vectr (Web/Win/Mac/Linux) – Simple online vector editor.
  • Sumopaint (Web) – Versatile online painting & editing.

3D Modeling, Animation & Sculpting

  • Blender (Win/Mac/Linux) – Full-featured 3D modeling, animation, sculpting.
  • SculptGL (Web) – Web-based 3D sculpting tool.

Animation & Storyboarding

  • OpenToonz (Win/Mac) – Professional 2D animation suite.
  • Pencil2D (Win/Mac/Linux) – Simple frame-by-frame 2D animation.
  • Synfig Studio (Win/Mac/Linux) – Vector-based 2D animation.
  • Storyboarder (Win/Mac/Linux) – Easy storyboarding for artists.
  • Krita (Win/Mac/Linux) – Also offers frame-by-frame animation.

Audio & Music Creation

  • Audacity (Win/Mac/Linux) – Multi-track audio recording/editing.
  • LMMS (Win/Mac/Linux) – Beat making & music production.
  • Cakewalk by BandLab (Win) – Professional DAW (free, BandLab account required).

Writing, Screenwriting & Publishing

  • Manuskript (Win/Mac/Linux) – Open-source tool for writers (novels/scripts).
  • Trelby (Win/Linux) – Free screenplay editor.
  • Scrite (Win/Mac/Linux) – Modern, feature-rich screenwriting app.
  • Scribus (Win/Mac/Linux) – Desktop publishing/layout (like InDesign).

Other Useful Tools

  • OBS Studio (Win/Mac/Linux) – Recording & streaming (great for art process).
  • PureRef (Win/Mac/Linux) – Reference image board for artists (free with optional donation).
  • Darktable (Win/Mac/Linux) – RAW photo development (like Lightroom).
  • Shotcut (Win/Mac/Linux) – Free video editor.
